What are Web3 Domains?

Web3 domains are blockchain-based domain names that provide true ownership, censorship resistance, and enhanced functionality. Unlike traditional domains, these are owned by you as NFTs and cannot be taken away by centralized authorities.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between ENS and Unstoppable Domains?

ENS requires annual renewal fees and is primarily for Ethereum, while Unstoppable Domains are one-time purchases that support 400+ cryptocurrencies and never expire.

Do I really own my Web3 domain?

Yes, Web3 domains are NFTs stored in your cryptocurrency wallet. As long as you control your wallet, you own the domain and no one can take it away.

Can I use Web3 domains for regular websites?

Yes, many Web3 domains can be used for regular websites through IPFS integration or traditional DNS setup, depending on the platform.

What happens if I lose my wallet?

If you lose access to your wallet without proper backups, you may lose your domain. We provide comprehensive security guidance to prevent this.
