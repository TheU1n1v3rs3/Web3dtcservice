What is RPA?

RPA (Robotic Process Automation) is a technology that uses software bots to automate repetitive business processes. These intelligent bots can perform the same tasks as humans but faster, more accurately, and without breaks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which processes are suitable for RPA?

RPA is ideal for repetitive, rule-based, high-volume processes. Examples include data entry, invoice processing, report generation, and email management.

How long does RPA implementation take?

Simple processes can be automated in 2-4 weeks, while complex processes may take 2-3 months. This timeframe includes analysis, development, testing, and deployment.

What is the ROI of RPA?

Most of our clients see ROI within 6-12 months. Typically, 60-80% cost savings and 5-10x productivity increase are achieved.

Do RPA bots require maintenance?

Yes, RPA bots need regular maintenance for system updates, process changes, and performance optimization. We provide comprehensive maintenance services.
